Ode to Jake's Bum

A dimple small, a curve so slight, Jake's tiny bum, a charming sight. No grand expanse, no sweeping round, Just neat perfection can be found. Like sculpted stone, a subtle grace, A gentle slope in its small space. A pocket-sized posterior, That banishes all gloom and fear. Though miniature, it holds its charm, A cozy haven, safe from harm. A testament that beauty lies, In subtle curves before our eyes. So let us praise this petite form, That weathers every social storm. Jake's perfect, tiny man bum, you see, A small wonder for all to agree.
